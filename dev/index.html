UrbanCanopy.jl

Documentation for UrbanCanopy.jl.

UrbanCanopy.jl implements urban canopy processes for the EarthSciML framework, including urban heat island effects, building energy balance, urban surface exchange, anthropogenic heat fluxes, and urban boundary layer interactions.
